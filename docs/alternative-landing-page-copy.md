## Process
### Eyebrow
How it works

### Headline
High-trust advisory with a deliberate sequence.

### Intro Body
The process is simple on purpose. The goal is not to overwhelm you with frameworks. It is to create enough clarity that the next move feels obvious.

### Steps
#### Step 01
- Title: Pressure-test the operating reality
- Body: Look at where the business is actually slowing down, what the owner is carrying manually, and where visibility is weak.

#### Step 02
- Title: Separate bottlenecks from tooling noise
- Body: Work out whether the constraint is process design, current-stack usage, staffing friction, or a genuinely good AI use case.

#### Step 03
- Title: Recommend the practical sequence
- Body: Set out the most sensible order of moves, including what should be parked, deferred, or avoided completely.

#### Step 04
- Title: Only scope implementation if it earns the right
- Body: If a tool change or build is justified, the next step becomes tighter and smaller because the decision groundwork has already been done.

## Design Direction Notes For The Alternative Concept
- Keep the page more visually led than copy led in the hero.
- Use serif only for the strategic message and major editorial moments.
- Use sans-serif for supporting body, labels, navigation, interface, and proof details.
- Preserve the founder-advisory positioning rather than shifting back toward generic AI services.
- Make the hero feel premium, modern, and intentional rather than warm, rustic, or agency-like.
- Use fewer sentences above the fold and let composition, photography, and visual systems carry more of the identity.
