# FinnAI Landing Direction Portfolio

## Direction 1 — The AI Efficiency Audit
**Audience:** Service businesses with admin drag and messy handoffs.
**Tone:** Editorial, credible, operational.
**Core promise:** In one day, get a clear map of where AI actually saves time and where it should stay out.
**Hero:** `A one-day AI efficiency audit for service businesses drowning in repetitive work.`
**Primary CTA:** Book a strategy call
**Trust style:** Founder-led, decision-ready, anti-hype
**Why it works:** Strongest fit with current page and easiest to sell quickly.
**Risk:** Can sound broad unless paired with clear niche examples.

## Direction 2 — The Missed-Revenue Angle
**Audience:** Mortgage brokers, accountants, bookkeeping firms, legal/admin-heavy service teams.
**Tone:** Commercial, no-nonsense, ROI-first.
**Core promise:** Stop losing leads and delivery time to slow follow-up, manual admin, and inconsistent operations.
**Hero:** `Fix the hidden admin drag that costs service firms leads, speed, and margin.`
**Primary CTA:** See if your workflow qualifies
**Trust style:** Bottleneck diagnosis, revenue leakage, speed to response
**Why it works:** Easier to connect to money; stronger for outbound.
**Risk:** Needs careful proof so it doesn’t sound like generic agency copy.

## Direction 3 — The Founder Advisory Angle
**Audience:** Owners who distrust agencies and don’t want a giant software rollout.
**Tone:** High-trust, strategic, selective.
**Core promise:** Independent AI advisory before you buy tools or commit to implementation.
**Hero:** `Before you buy more software, get an operator’s view of what your business actually needs.`
**Primary CTA:** Talk it through with Finlay
**Trust style:** Independent recommendations, practical sequencing, no vendor bias
**Why it works:** Differentiates from tool resellers and automation generalists.
**Risk:** Slightly softer conversion energy unless paired with stronger outcomes.

## Direction 4 — The Melbourne Niche Sprint
**Audience:** Melbourne-based professional and local service firms.
**Tone:** Local, practical, fast-to-deploy.
**Core promise:** Local firms can get a fast AI workflow diagnosis without a bloated transformation project.
**Hero:** `Melbourne service businesses: find the workflows AI should fix first.`
**Primary CTA:** Request availability this week
**Trust style:** Local relevance, tailored audit, founder-led delivery
**Why it works:** Best for targeted outbound and niche-specific pages.
**Risk:** Narrows top-of-funnel unless used as a variant page or campaign page.

## Recommended Path
1. Keep **Direction 1** as the core site.
2. Borrow the **commercial sharpness of Direction 2** in the hero/subheads and outreach.
3. Use **Direction 4** for campaign-specific variants and outbound messaging.
4. Keep **Direction 3** as trust language throughout the page and sales calls.
