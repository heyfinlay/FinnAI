# FinnAI Lead Qualification Rubric

Score each category 1–5. Prioritise leads scoring **18+**.

## 1. Workflow complexity
- **1:** Very simple single-person workflow
- **3:** Multiple recurring steps, some internal coordination
- **5:** Multi-step service delivery, handoffs, compliance/admin load

## 2. Lead-handling urgency
- **1:** Low inbound volume, low urgency
- **3:** Moderate lead flow, some follow-up friction
- **5:** Revenue clearly depends on fast enquiry response and consistent follow-up

## 3. Admin drag
- **1:** Minimal repetitive admin
- **3:** Some duplicated communication or manual tracking
- **5:** Heavy repetitive admin across onboarding, fulfilment, updates, or reporting

## 4. Ability to pay
- **1:** Tiny operator, low commercial upside
- **3:** Healthy small business
- **5:** Established service firm where a workflow gain has real monetary value

## 5. Founder pain visibility
- **1:** No visible bottleneck
- **3:** Some team/process strain implied
- **5:** Clear owner bottleneck, hiring pressure, growth strain, or service inconsistency risk

## 6. Ease of reaching decision-maker
- **1:** Hard to identify or access
- **3:** Reachable but indirect
- **5:** Founder/partner/direct contact is obvious and active

## Qualification notes
**Strong fit signs**
- Reviews mention responsiveness, admin, coordination, or communication
- The business has multiple staff and a high-touch client process
- The offer involves intake, documents, quoting, approvals, or recurring client updates

**Weak fit signs**
- Commodity low-margin work with little workflow depth
- Extremely early-stage operators with no process to improve yet
- Businesses looking only for a cheap tool recommendation
